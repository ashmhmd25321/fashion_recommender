What is Sustainable Fashion?

Sustainable fashion is an approach to fashion that maximizes benefits to people and communities while minimizing impact on the environment. This includes addressing the whole system of fashion, from the growing or production of fibers to the continued use and disposal of products.

Using environmentally friendly materials
Reducing waste and pollution
Supporting fair labor practices
Creating durable, long-lasting products
Promoting circular economy principles

Tips for Sustainable Fashion

Buy Less, Choose Well

Invest in quality pieces that will last longer rather than fast fashion items that quickly wear out.

Second-Hand Shopping

Explore thrift stores, vintage shops, and online resale platforms to give existing clothes a second life.

Care for Your Clothes

Wash clothes less frequently, at lower temperatures, and line dry when possible to extend their lifespan.

Research Brands

Support brands that are transparent about their supply chain and committed to sustainable practices.

Repair and Upcycle

Learn basic mending skills or find a local tailor to repair items instead of discarding them.

Rent for Special Occasions

Consider renting outfits for special events rather than buying something you'll only wear once.

Making sustainable fashion choices helps reduce environmental impact and supports ethical production practices across the global supply chain.
